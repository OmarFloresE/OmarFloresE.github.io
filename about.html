Some Backstory

I graduated in May 2021 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Fresno State University. It was an extraordinary time to enter the job market, with the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic. Despite the circumstances, I secured a role as an Advisor/Web Support Associate at Microsoft, which relocated me to the heart of Silicon Valley. While at Microsoft, I found myself immersed in the daily grind of a 9-to-5, leaving little time for my true passions and aspirations. However, fate took an unexpected turn as I, along with my entire team, faced a recent layoff, a fate shared by thousands in the tech industry. This seemingly unfortunate event turned out to be a blessing in disguise. It reignited my passion for programming and cybersecurity, reminding me of my true calling!
